Doggiie Dog World Has Been Developed To Be A One-Stop Destination For Pets, More Like A Pet Mall – A One-Stop Pet Services And Wellness Centre.

A very special and personalised pet centre Doggiie Dog World has been a long awaited dream for owner Pooja Advani.

Having worked at and managed different grooming salons internationally, Pooja decided to translate her passion for animals by bringing the international concept of a luxury dog grooming salon in the form of Doggiie Dog World to Mumbai, India. Pooja’s love for her dogs, Bobo and Baloo was the primary inspiration behind Doggiie Dog World and the idea of exploring different avenues in the pet industry.

Passionate about animals since a young, age, she soon realised that her true calling lay in working with them. In 2010 Pooja moved to Singapore to educate herself in various aspects of pet care and received certification as Certified Groomer, Dog Trainer & K9 Behaviorist, Hydrotherapist, and Quantum-Touch PractitionerShe worked for 3 years in Singapore to get first hand experienceas a Specialist - Canine Cosmetology & Trainer. To widen her horizon of knowledge she went to London to train further as a Hydrotherapist. Pooja is a registered Hydrotherapist for small animals from NARCH UK.
