Grooming Is An Essential Part Of Pet Care And Helps To Enhance The Pet's Hygiene And Appearance.

The best time to start grooming your pet is after it has had all its vaccinations, which is approximately at the age of 3 months. The sooner you introduce your pet to professional grooming, the happier it will be, removing any fear it may tend to develop. All pets require daily grooming; the kind and style of grooming will depend on their breed, age and health.

Benefits Of Daily Grooming:

  • Reduces the likelihood of various skin problems and infestation of parasites.
  • Monitors the pet's health by checking for cuts, swelling, limps or changes in temperature, all of which are indicative of illness.
